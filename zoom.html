    Cover Letter
    About me
    Hi, I’m Jason Curescu. I spent the last seven years as a senior product designer at Mogul, where I helped redesign our recruiter platform and built features that made hiring easier. Now that the company has closed, I’m looking for a new team where I can keep learning, solve real problems, and design things that help people. Zoom’s mission and the work you’re doing really stood out to me, and I’d be excited to be part of it.
    My connection to Zoom
    Zoom has a special place in my life. When my wife and I were living alone in New York during the pandemic and had our first child, it was how we stayed close to our family. We had big Zoom calls where we’d catch up, play games, and just feel a little less isolated. It made a hard time feel a bit more normal, and I’ve always been grateful for that. That personal connection is a big reason why I’d be so excited to contribute to the product and help others feel that same sense of closeness.
    Value I bring
    I’m a product designer who likes making complicated things feel simple. At Mogul, I led the redesign of our recruiter platform and worked closely with engineers to build AI features that actually helped people get hired. I like being involved from the start, figuring out what’s really needed, and working with others to bring ideas to life. I’ve led projects, mentored teammates, and I’m not afraid to dig into messy problems. I care a lot about clear interactions, thoughtful design, and creating tools that feel easy to use. Zoom feels like the kind of place where I could do my best work and keep growing.
