    Cover Letter
    About me
    I’m a Senior Product Designer based in New York City with over 7 years of experience designing consumer and desktop products. I’ve worked on fast-moving teams where design plays a big role in shaping product strategy. At Mogul, I’ve led the redesign of our recruiter platform, built our design system, and collaborated closely with engineers to ship high-quality features across web and mobile. Before that, I co-founded and launched a public-facing tool that helped over a million people connect with local food during the early months of the pandemic.
    Copilot and me
    I’m really excited about Copilot’s mission to make personal finance less stressful. Money is such a personal and emotional part of people’s lives, and good design can make a big difference in how people feel about it. I also want to say how much I enjoyed exploring your website. It’s beautifully designed, intuitive, and honestly just fun to click through. It pulled me in right away and made me want to try the app. That kind of experience really stuck with me.
    Value I bring
    I bring strong experience designing across platforms, a deep understanding of systems thinking, and a sharp eye for interaction and visual design. I care about clarity, speed, and how products feel day to day. I’m comfortable running my own projects, working across disciplines, and learning directly from users. I’m also based in NYC and happy to be in the office regularly.

    Thanks for taking the time to read this. I’d love to talk more about how I could help support Copilot’s mission and design great tools for people who want more control over their money.
