    About me
    I’m a senior product designer based in New York City. Over the past few years, I’ve been focused on building tools that help people work smarter and with more confidence. Most recently, I led the redesign of an AI-powered recruiting platform at Mogul. I also built our design system from the ground up and worked closely with engineers to ship features that made hiring easier and more inclusive. I care a lot about clean, intuitive design and I like digging into complex problems to find simple, practical solutions.
    Coast and me
    What got me excited about Coast is how you're modernizing payments for fleet businesses. It’s one of those industries that’s long overdue for better tools, and I love that you're stepping in with something thoughtful and well designed. I’m drawn to companies that rethink how things should work, not just how they’ve always been done. Coast feels like the kind of team that’s not afraid to roll up their sleeves and build something meaningful, and that’s exactly what I’m looking for.
    Value I bring
    I bring a mix of strong visual design, product thinking, and real-world execution. I’ve led big projects, worked across teams, and shipped things that made a real impact. I like working fast without cutting corners, and I’m comfortable jumping into ambiguity and figuring things out. I’d be excited to help Coast grow and bring more clarity and ease to the way your customers work.

    Thanks for reading. I’d love to chat more and see how I can support the Coast team.
