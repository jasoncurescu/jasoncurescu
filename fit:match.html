    Cover Letter
    About me
    I’m a senior product designer with over 8 years of experience creating tools that help people work smarter and feel more confident in what they’re doing. Most recently, I led design at Mogul, an AI-powered hiring platform. I redesigned our recruiter dashboard, built the first design system, and worked side by side with engineers to launch features that made hiring faster and more inclusive. I’ve also co-founded a platform that helped people find local farms during the pandemic. It ended up reaching over a million searches in just a few months.
    Fit:Match and me
    Fit:Match feels like the kind of place where I could do my best work. I’m excited by the idea of using tech and data to make shopping feel more personal and less frustrating. Helping people find what fits them without all the guesswork just makes sense, and I’d love to be part of a team that’s building something that actually helps people feel better in their own skin. Your focus on real solutions over hype really speaks to me.
    Value I bring
    I’m quick to jump in, ask the right questions, and figure out what matters most. I’ve worked across full product flows, from messy idea to launch. I care a lot about clean interfaces, real user needs, and making sure design fits within the real world of tech and timelines. I’m comfortable leading projects, collaborating with teams, and refining things until they feel just right. If you’re looking for someone who’s thoughtful, hands-on, and ready to contribute right away, I’d love to talk.

    Thanks for reading. I’d love to chat more and see how I can support the Fit:Match team.
