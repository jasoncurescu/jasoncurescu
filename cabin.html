    About me
    I’m a senior product designer who loves working on tools that simplify complex problems. I’ve spent the last few years leading design at Mogul, an AI-driven recruitment platform, where I helped rebuild our recruiter dashboard from the ground up. I also created our first design system and worked closely with engineers to launch features that made hiring easier and faster. I’m a big believer in user-centered design, and I like to dig deep into the details to make sure everything feels clear, intuitive, and useful. I enjoy solving messy problems and turning them into experiences that just make sense.
    Value I bring
    I bring a mix of hands-on design experience and a thoughtful, collaborative approach to every project. I’m comfortable leading projects from discovery through launch, working with cross-functional teams to align on goals and make smart decisions along the way. I’ve worked closely with strategists, engineers, and end users to build AI-powered tools that create real results. I also enjoy mentoring other designers and helping teams stay focused on what actually matters to the people we’re designing for. I’d be excited to bring that same energy to Cabin, helping clients move their ideas forward and building digital experiences that truly work.
