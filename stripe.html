    About me
    I’m a product designer who’s spent the last few years working on tools that support complex workflows and help people feel more confident in what they’re doing. Most recently I was the lead designer at Mogul, an AI-driven recruitment platform. I redesigned our recruiter dashboard from the ground up, built our first design system, and worked closely with engineering to ship new features that made hiring faster and easier for our users. I care a lot about the details, love creating clean and intuitive interfaces, and enjoy solving tricky problems by listening to real people and seeing what they actually need.
    Stripe and me
    Stripe is a perfect example of a product that makes complicated things easier, which is what originally drew me in. I’m excited about the chance to contribute as a Product Designer to something that helps businesses grow and operate more smoothly. That kind of impact is exactly what motivates me.
    Value I bring
    I bring 8+ years of experience, including leading redesigns, launching features, and collaborating closely with product and engineering from idea to launch. I’ve worked across multiple product areas, always focused on clarity, usefulness, and real-world impact. I enjoy figuring out how things connect and making them better, especially when the problem is messy or unclear. I ask questions, test ideas, and keep things grounded in what people actually need.
    Thanks for reading. I’d love to chat more and see how I can support the Stripe team.
